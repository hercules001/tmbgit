# Quickstart

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Launch the App

Open the TMB app in your mobile browser. Since TMB is a Progressive Web App (PWA), we recommend adding it to your home screen for a native feel:

* iOS Safari: Tap the Share icon > "Add to Home Screen"
* Android Chrome: Tap the Three-dot menu > "Install App"
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Create Your Account

Tap Get Started and sign in using your Email, Google, or X (Twitter) account. Powered by Privy, TMB creates a secure wallet for you instantly. No extensions required.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### One-Tap Wallet Setup

The first time you go to trade, you’ll see a one-time setup modal. Simply tap Continue. TMB handles the "boring" stuff in the background: deploying your Safe Smart Account, generating credentials, and setting up approvals. When you see "Let's Go!", you’re officially on-chain.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Fund The Wallet

To trade, you’ll need USDC. You can deposit directly or bridge funds from Polygon, Base, or Arbitrum.

> See [Deposits & Withdrawls ](../wallet-and-funds/deposits-and-withdrawals.md)for a step-by-step on moving assets.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Pick Your Play

Explore the Home screen to find a market that fits your vibe. Use the category tabs at the top to filter the action:

* Crypto: Ultra-fast 5-minute and 15-minute price prediction markets.
* IPL / Sports: Live and upcoming matches with real-time odds.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Place Your First Trade

Once you’ve picked a side, it’s time to move. We’ve replaced clunky buttons with tactile action triggers:

* Hold YES: If you’re betting on the outcome happening.
* Hold NO: If you’re betting against it.

Hold for roughly one second. You’ll see a progress bar fill up—once it hits the edge, you’ll feel a haptic pulse confirming your trade is live.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Track Your Positions

Once your trade is confirmed, the card automatically flips to reveal your "Live View." Here, you can track your position size, real-time odds, and access the Sell button if you want to exit early.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Track Your Portfolio

Want the big picture? Head to the Profile tab. This is your command center for active positions, historical wins, and redeeming your payouts.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
