# FAQ

### General

<details>

<summary>What is TMB?</summary>

TMB (Trust Me Bro) is a high-speed prediction market app. We’ve optimized the trading experience for mobile, allowing you to trade on crypto prices, sports, and culture with a single tactile gesture.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Is TMB free?</summary>

Browsing and account setup are free. We charge a transparent 1% platform fee only on Buy orders. Selling and Redeeming are free.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I install TMB as an app?</summary>

Yes. TMB is a Progressive Web App (PWA).

* iOS: Safari > Share > Add to Home Screen
* Android: Chrome > Menu > Install App

</details>

***

### Technical & Troubleshooting

<details>

<summary>Why is the app stuck on the loading screen or feeling laggy?</summary>

TMB relies on high-speed data feeds to keep your trades real-time. If you are stuck in the authentication phase or the interface feels slow:

* Switch your connection region
* Refresh the app or relaunch the PWA

</details>

***

### Account & Wallet

<details>

<summary>Do I need a seed phrase or MetaMask?</summary>

No. When you sign up via Email, Google, or X, TMB automatically creates an embedded wallet for you. No extensions required.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Where are my funds stored?</summary>

Your USDC.e is held in a Safe Smart Account on the Polygon blockchain. TMB is non-custodial; we never have access to your private keys or your funds.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What is the Safe Wallet setup?</summary>

The first time you trade, you’ll be prompted to "Activate" your account. This deploys your personal Safe Smart Account on-chain, which handles all your trading and security.

</details>

***

### Trading

<details>

<summary>How can I get started with my first prediction?</summary>

Select a market and press and hold the Action Block (YES/NO or Team Name). A progress bar will fill-once it hits the top and you feel the haptic "thump," your prediction is live.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How is my payout calculated?</summary>

TMB does the math for you. The number you see in the Action Block is your Potential Payout based on your Preset trade amount.

* Exampl&#x65;_:_ If your preset is $1 and the odds are 50%, the block will show a \*\*$2.00\*\* payout.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I cancel a trade mid-hold?</summary>

Yes. If you let go before the progress bar is full, the trade is cancelled instantly.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I exit a trade early?</summary>

Absolutely. Tap any active position in your Profile and hit Sell to exit at the current market price.

</details>

***

### Funding & Withdrawals

<details>

<summary>What currency do I need?</summary>

TMB uses USDC.e on Polygon. You can deposit USDC from Polygon, Base, or Arbitrum, and we’ll handle the conversion automatically.

</details>

<details>

<summary>How do I get my money out?</summary>

Go to Profile, tap Withdraw, and enter any valid Polygon (0x) address.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Why is my "Withdraw" button disabled?</summary>

You must complete the one-time wallet setup (triggered by your first trade) before you can withdraw funds.

</details>

***

### Results & Payouts

<details>

<summary>How do I get paid when I win?</summary>

Winning positions move to the Redeem tab in your Profile. You must tap Redeem (or Redeem All) to sweep those winnings back into your tradable balance.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Who decides who won?</summary>

We use the UMA Optimistic Oracle. It’s a decentralized "truth machine" that ensures market resolutions are transparent, fair, and cannot be manipulated by TMB.

</details>
