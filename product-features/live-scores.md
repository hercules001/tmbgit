# Live Scores

### Match-day Tracking: IPL

During the IPL season, our market cards transform into live scoreboards featuring:

* Official Branding: Team logos and colors (MI, CSK, RCB, etc.) for instant recognition.
* Real-time Stats: Runs, wickets, and overs updated the moment they happen.
* Momentum Shifts: Live match status—from Upcoming lineups to Ended results.

***

### The Lifecycle of a Match

| Status   | What you see                                                 |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Upcoming | Match start time and countdown.                              |
| Live     | Ball-by-ball score, overs, and current batting/bowling side. |
| Ended    | Final scoreline with the winner clearly highlighted.         |

***

### Score-Driven Trading

We’ve bridged the gap between the scoreboard and the market. Tap any live match card to open the All Markets drawer. This allows you to:

1. Watch the score move in real-time.
2. Predict the shift (e.g., _Who wins the toss_ or _Total runs_).
3. Trigger the trade instantly based on the match momentum.

***

### 💡 Quick Specs

* Auto-Refresh: Scores are pulled from high-speed cricket APIs and refresh automatically. No manual reloading required.
