# Profile & Portfolio

### Performance Overview

The header provides an immediate snapshot of your account health:

* Balance: Your tradable USDC.e. Tap Add Funds to top up instantly.
* Win Rate & Bets: High-level stats showing your accuracy and total activity.
* P\&L: Your cumulative realized profit or loss.

***

### Position Management

Your trades are sorted into three high-speed toggles:

{% tabs %}
{% tab title="📈 Active" %}
Your live "in-play" trades.

* Real-time Feeds: Prices and Unrealized P\&L update via WebSocket-watch the green and red shift as the market moves.
* Sell Trigger: Tap any active card to access the Sell button. This allows you to exit at the current market price to lock in gains or cut a loss before the market settles.
{% endtab %}

{% tab title="🕒 Closed" %}
Your permanent track record. Once a market resolves, it moves here to show your Realized P\&L and final outcome (Won/Lost).
{% endtab %}

{% tab title="🎁 Redeem" %}
The payout hub. When you predict correctly, your shares move here to be converted into USDC.e.

* Redeem: Claim a single win.
* Redeem All: One-tap batch redemption. TMB bundles your wins into a single transaction for maximum efficiency.
* Celebration: A successful redemption triggers a confetti celebration as the funds hit your balance.
{% endtab %}
{% endtabs %}

***

### Features & Tips

* Share Cards: Proud of a win? Tap any closed position to generate a custom Share Card for social media.
* Redeem Habit: Resolved markets don't auto-payout. Check the Redeem tab after every match keep your balance fueled.
* Total P\&L: Note that the P\&L in your profile header only accounts for Realized (closed) positions.
