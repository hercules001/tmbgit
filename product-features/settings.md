# Settings

### Accessing Settings

Tap the gear icon ⚙️ on the sidebar which is accessed by clicking profile image in the header or navigate via the Profile screen. Settings are synced to your account so your preferences follow you across devices.

***

### Preset Trade Amount

This is your "Default Stake." Every time you trigger a trade, this is the amount TMB uses to calculate your potential payout.

* Quick Select: One-tap presets for $1, $5, $10, $25, $50, or $100.
* Custom Amount: Enter any stake from $0.01 to $1,000,000 for total control.
* Auto-Save: Your selection is saved instantly. You’ll see your active preset displayed at the top of the Home screen as well.

Reminder: Fee not included in preset.

***

### Feedback & Sensory

Customize how the app "feels" during the Action Trigger:

| Toggle  | Experience                                                   |
| ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Sounds  | Audio cues for trade confirmations and app interactions.     |
| Haptics | Tactile vibration pulses that build as you hold the trigger. |
