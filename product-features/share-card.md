# Share Card

### Anatomy of a Card

Every card is a custom-generated snapshot of your play:

* The Vibe: Choose from a gallery of dynamic background images to match your mood (e.g., cityscapes, parties, or gaming setups).
* The Verdict: Displays a clear header like _"Greetings from the winning side"_ for the wins.
* The Stats: Summarizes the market, your team/side, Invested vs. Returned amounts, and your ROI %.
* The Proof: Includes the TMB branding and the exact date of the trade.

***

### How to Share

1. Select: Go to Profile > Closed and tap any position.
2. Customize: Tap the Share button. Use the carousel to pick the background image that fits your win.
3. Broadcast: Tap Share on X to post instantly, or Copy Image to paste it into WhatsApp, Instagram, or Discord.
