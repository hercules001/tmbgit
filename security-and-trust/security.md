# Security

### 1. The Non-Custodial Model

TMB is a high-performance interface, not a bank.

* Your Keys: Managed via Privy. Whether you use an embedded wallet or an external one (like MetaMask or Phantom), we never see your seed phrase or private keys.
* Your Funds: Stored in a Safe Smart Account (formerly Gnosis Safe) on the Polygon blockchain.
* The Settlement: All predictions happen on Polymarket’s audited smart contracts. TMB simply routes your signed orders to the global order book.

***

### 2. Wallet Protection: Privy & MPC

We use Privy for seamless, secure onboarding.

* MPC Technology: For embedded wallets, your private key is never stored in one place. It is "sharded" using Multi-Party Computation (MPC), meaning no single breach can ever compromise your wallet.
* Self-Custody: You can export your private key at any time. You aren't locked into TMB; your assets belong to you on the blockchain.

***

### 3. The Safe Advantage

Every TMB user predicts through a Safe Smart Account. This is the gold standard for on-chain security.

* Smart Permissions: The Safe ensures that only _you_ can authorize predictions or withdrawals.
* Gasless Execution: The Safe allows TMB to handle the technical "gas" fees behind the scenes, so you don't have to manage MATIC balances while staying secure.
* Immutable Logic: The rules of the Safe contract are set on Polygon and cannot be changed by TMB or any third party.

***

### 4. Secure Authentication (SIWE)

We use Sign-In with Ethereum (SIWE).

* No Passwords: You don't have a "TMB Password" that can be leaked or brute-forced.
* Cryptographic Proof: Every session is authorized by a unique, one-time signature from your wallet. This binds your session to your specific device and the `app.tmb.fun` domain, preventing "man-in-the-middle" attacks.

***

### 5. Protocol-Level Truth (UMA)

Market results are not decided by TMB employees. We rely on the UMA Optimistic Oracle.

* Decentralized Resolution: Outcomes are proposed and verified by a global network of voters.
* Dispute Logic: If a result is wrong, it can be disputed on-chain. This ensures that even if TMB disappeared tomorrow, the "truth" of the market remains verifiable and your winnings redeemable.

***

### 6. Your Security Checklist

While we build the fortress, you hold the gate. Follow these rules to stay 100% safe:

* ✅ Verify the URL: Only predict on `app.tmb.fun`. Bookmark it.
* ✅ Be Phish-Proof: TMB will never DM you asking for a seed phrase, a private key, or a "wallet verification" signature.
