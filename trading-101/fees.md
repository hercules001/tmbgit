# Fees

### Fee Summary

| Fee Type       | Amount  | Application                                        |
| -------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Platform Fee   | 1%      | Charged on every Buy order (Action Trigger).       |
| Gas Fees       | $0.00   | Covered by our Safe Smart Account architecture.    |
| Redemption Fee | $0.00   | Claiming your winnings is always free.             |
| Selling Fee    | $0.00   | Exiting a position early is free of platform fees. |
| Bridging Fee   | Minimal | Only applies on other networks except polygon.     |

***

### The Platform Fee (1%)

TMB charges a flat 1% fee on the stake of every buy order. This fee covers the high-speed order routing, real-time data streaming, and the non-custodial infrastructure that keeps your trades secure.

#### How it Works

When you trigger a trade, the fee is added to your stake.

* Your Preset trade amount: $10.00
* Platform Fee (1%): $0.10
* Total deducted from wallet: $10.10

> ⚠️ Important: Your wallet balance must cover the Trade Amount + 1% Fee. If you have exactly $1.00 in your wallet and try to place a $1.00 prediction, the transaction will fail. Always keep a small "buffer" for fees.

***

### Gas & Execution

On many platforms, you have to worry about holding MATIC to pay for blockchain gas. Not here.

TMB uses Safe Smart Accounts to abstract gas fees away. When you hold an Action Trigger, the backend handles the transaction costs on the Polygon network. You never have to worry about gas prices or failed transactions due to low MATIC balances.

***

### Market Dynamics (Spread)

Polymarket is a Central Limit Order Book (CLOB). Like any market, there is a small "spread" between the current price of YES and NO.

* This isn't a TMB fee; it’s a standard market mechanic that compensates liquidity providers.
* You don't pay this separately-t is already baked into the Potential Payout you see in your Action Blocks.

***

### Bridging & Deposits

* Polygon Deposits: 100% free. What you send is what you get.
* Base/Arbitrum Deposits: Our bridging partner (Relay) charges a small network fee to move your USDC across chains. This fee is disclosed by the bridge provider during the deposit process.
