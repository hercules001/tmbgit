# Manage Positions

#### 📈 Active

These are your live trades. Every card here updates in real-time as market odds shift.

* Early Exit: If you want to lock in a profit or minimize a loss before the market resolves, tap the position and hit Sell.

***

#### 🕒 Closed

A record of your completed trades. This shows your final result (Win/Loss) and the total P\&L for each specific event.

***

#### 🎁 Redeem

When you win, your shares move here.

* Claiming: Winning shares are worth $1.00 each. Tap claim on a position (or Redeem All) to sweep those winnings into your balance.

***

### Tips

* **Check redeem regularly** — Resolved markets don't auto-redeem. Visit the Redeem tab to collect your winnings.
* **Selling is not redeeming** — Selling exits an active position at market price. Redeeming collects the full $1.00 per winning share after resolution.
