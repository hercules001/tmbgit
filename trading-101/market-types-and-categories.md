TMB provides high-velocity price prediction markets for users who prioritize immediate results and volatility.

* The Format: Predictions are structured as: _"Will \[Asset] stay above $\[Price] at \[Time]?"_

{% tab title="🏏 Cricket (IPL)" %}
Access comprehensive prediction markets for the Indian Premier League with deep data integration.

* Real-Time Data: Market cards feature live scoreboards and team branding, allowing you to monitor the match and the market simultaneously.
* Match Hubs: Each match serves as a primary entry point to a broader "All Markets" drawer, which includes:
  * Match Winner: Predict the outright winner. Outcomes are represented by the Team Names (e.g., MI vs. PBKS).
  * The Draw: In specific markets, a Draw option is available as a third outcome for predictions where a stalemate is possible.
  * The Toss: A rapid-resolution market based on the pre-match coin flip.
  * Event-Specific Predictions: Trade on outcomes like Total Runs, Team Top Batter, or Most Sixes.

### Market Structure

| Property         | Definition                                                                 |
| ---------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Question         | The specific event or price point being predicted.                         |
| Outcome Options  | The available choices (e.g., Yes/No, Team Names, or Draw).                 |
| Current Odds     | The market-implied probability of an outcome occurring.                    |
| Potential Payout | The total return based on your current preset stake and the current price. |
| Resolution       | The timestamp or event conclusion when the market is finalized.            |

#### Key Market Styles

1. Binary: Most markets offer two outcomes (e.g., Yes/No or Team A/Team B). The combined price of these outcomes typically equals $1.00.
2. Multi-Outcome: For complex predictions, such as "Top Batter" or matches with a "Draw" possibility, the card will display all available outcomes. You can select any outcome to view its specific odds and potential payout.
