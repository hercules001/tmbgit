# The Action Trigger

### How It Works: The "Hold to Trade"

The Action Trigger is a deliberate move. It ensures you never place a trade by accident while providing a satisfying haptic "hit" when your position goes live.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/IMG_1816 (2).jpg" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

| Action               | What Happens                                            |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Hold YES / Team Name | You believe the outcome will happen.                    |
| Hold NO / Team Name  | You believe the outcome won't happen.                   |
| Release Early        | The trade is immediately cancelled. No funds are moved. |

The Process: Press and hold your chosen Action Block. A progress bar will fill inside the block. Once it reaches the top (approx. 1 second), you'll feel a haptic pulse—your trade is now live on the blockchain.
